Restoring a classic

Saving a part of automotive history, thats our mission. From our premises in Belgium, our small team of passionate car enthousiasts is working to restore a unique car, an Isotta Fraschini publicity car used by the Martini corporation in the early twentienth century, to its former glory.

To this end, our team is always on on the lookout for new, passionate people and businesses to contribute to our project. Take a look at our site and discover how you can help.

Everybody can help

Although the restoration has come a long way already, we still have much work to do. We can't do it alone however. To further our project, we need more documentation and specialiazed craftsmen. For this we call out to you, and ask for your help. If you're interested to aid our team, you can find more information on our renovation page!

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us. There is a multitude of ways in which anybody can aid the team, from sponsoring to providing services!
