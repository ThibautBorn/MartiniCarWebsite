Current objectives

Currently our main priority is to restore the car to a working condidition. To this end, we're looking for people who can help us with:

  • the car:
    • revision of the old engine
    • searching for an authentic or custom made chassis
    • restoring the interior
  • media: Save for some pictures saved by some local newpapers and archives, there is barely any information about the car. Therefore we're asking for any archive, company, library or collector to reach out to us if they have any information so we won't lose this unique story.

If you believe you can contribute to any of these goals, don't don't hesitate to contact us..

Long Term goals

In the long term we want to car to it's original 30's desing. This goal comes with it's own challenges like:

  • media: specific media about the earliest uses of the car is very rare and would be most welcome.
  • fabricating a new decorative Martini bottle
  • the search for original Viotti parts

Already completed

Although we still have a long road ahead of us, we've already made progress! Here you can take a look back at our progress:

  • restoration of the coachwork by a local mechanic
  • installation of the new doors and headlights
  • placing an authentic Viotti shield

As you can see, we're always getting closer to completing our goals. So don't hesitate to join us!
